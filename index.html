Install
Customer install flow
  1. Go to make.powerapps.com and open the target environment.
  2. Open Solutions, then choose Import solution.
  3. Upload the managed (production) or unmanaged (customizable) zip from this page.
  4. Complete import and publish changes when prompted.
  5. Open the Service Toolkit app once in Studio to confirm edit-mode load.
Prerequisites: Customer Service, Sales, and Product Management. Field Service and Marketing are not required for this release.
